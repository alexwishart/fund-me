Speedy Shopping

Having problems finding what to wear? Speedy Shopping is for you! This quick and easy app will suggest shops and brands near you, based on who you are, what you like wearing and what is in fashion. Just answer a few simple questions and find the perfect clothes for you, without the hassle of looking around the shops!

How is Speedy Shopping unique?

This app uses Watson based technology to learn your preferences and find the perfect style of clothes for each individual person.

How will this app benefit me?

Speedy shopping will help those who find it difficult and boring when shopping, because the app finds the clothes for you. This makes shopping stress free and you don't have to look around because we even tell you the nearest shop!
