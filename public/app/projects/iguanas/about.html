About CyberControl

CyberControl is a anti-bulling app focusing on cyber bullying. We have a range of tools including a confidencial and anonomus chat room where you can talk about all of you problems,troubles and worries. We also have 'A Steps to Take Page' to help you make changes. There is always specialist advice on offer that you won't get anywhere else. CyberControls helps all ages but is especially aimed at teenagers.
