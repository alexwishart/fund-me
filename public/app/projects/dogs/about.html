R.O.C.C

Relief, Organisation, Coordination and Control

By Data Dogs

R.O.C.C is a technologically advanced peice of equiptment to help people in places of natural disaster.

It is composed of a free app, signal boasting drones and a radio transmitter which sends a sigal to the

app carrier with the desired message. This provides an easy way for people to organise relief and aid

for disaster stricken people. It uses a simple symbol system that can be understood world wide, this

enables quick and effective relief work for those affected. This also creates a less stressful way to

organise the help for people and their families during times of hardship
