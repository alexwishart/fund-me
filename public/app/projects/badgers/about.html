Bluetooth Badgers

The Micro-Finder could help owners across the world re-unite with their 4 legged friends faster with more luck. If we gain the money to produce our product, for every product sold we will donate $10 to helping animal shelters across the world fund and also keep lost pets out of them and with their owners.

Our product is targeted to help dog owners world wide through the means of a tracking chip for in the scenario that their dog becomes lost, it can be located and tracked via an app installed which locks on to the tracker.
