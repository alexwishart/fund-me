Mobile Meals!

What is it?

Mobile meals is a fast,easy and tasty way for you to enjoy your food at home. No comlicated preparation, or frantic food buying is needed when you use Mobile meals.Its an app and hi-tech food storage. It helps people with disabilities be more independent and be able to cook for themselves. The app is simple and quick to use. You just choose your meal and it sends the list of ingredients to the machine. Then you just add water, cook in the oven and enjoy. Also the app can read out the ingredients and meal choices for anyone who is visually impaired. The app can change the meal choices for vegetarians, vegans etc.

Why buy our products?

It will give people with disabilities more independence, people can try lots of new dishes and it will make cooking a lot less of a hassle.
