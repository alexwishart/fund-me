Mobile Monkeys

shopbot is a helpful little robot found in all your favourite shops to aid with your shopping. It can give directions to the shop or direct you around the shop, all you need to do is tell your shopbot where you want to go or what item you want and it will find the way.

Shopbot will hand you the item you want with its extending arm and give you a short description. If you dont want it, your bot doesn't either. He will put it back and continue looking.
